Problem identification

COVID-19 is only amplifying what we’ve always known; that safe, stable housing is part of the foundation that creates health and wellness. When that foundation becomes shaky,   

  • whether it’s from not being able to afford monthly payments or utilities 

  • whether it’s from being stuck at home with family members with unresolved/ unaddressed trauma who may inflict violence 

  • whether it’s from being unhoused or having to live in crowded settings like nursing homes, shelters, or prisons 

  • or whether it’s from living in a racially or economically segregated neighborhood 

...the instability of this foundation is affecting our physical and mental health, as well as other support structures we need to thrive.
